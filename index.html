Welcome to my portfolio!

Below is a selection of projects I completed for CS1300 "User Interfaces and User Experience". As someone without prior design experience, this class was definitely out of my comfort zone. At the same time, I'm a strong believer that its our obligation to be as well-rounded and educated as possible.

Having design, accessibility, and user-centric thinking skills is useful for all areas of life. As such, my main goal in this class was to take on a wide range of projects, ranging from physical interfaces to poetry-finding web applications.

I can be better described as a data scientist with a passion for social entrepeneurship. My design portfolio for CS1300 reflects my desire to convey information in a clean and easily accessible manner.
